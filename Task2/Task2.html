Teerthanker Mahaveer University

TMU, established in 2008 in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the leading private universities in India. With a lush green campus and modern infrastructure, TMU offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields like engineering, management, medical sciences, pharmacy, law, and computer applications. The university is recognized by UGC and professional bodies like AICTE, BCI, and NMC. TMU stands out for its academic excellence, career-oriented education, industry interaction, and value-based learning rooted in Jain philosophy.

CCSIT Courses

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications provides students with a strong foundation in computer programming, software development, web technologies, and databases. Perfect for tech-savvy aspirants aiming for a career in IT.

B.Sc (CS)

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science offers an in-depth study of programming languages, computer networks, algorithms, and data structures. Ideal for students inclined toward theoretical and practical computing.

B.Tech (CSE)

The Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering is a comprehensive program that blends computer fundamentals, AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and hands-on lab training for real-world software development.

MCA

The Master of Computer Applications focuses on advanced computing concepts, software engineering, system management, and project development. Aimed at graduates who want to become IT professionals or pursue research.
